Working Papers

The Effects of Medicaid Expansion on Uninsured Rates [paper]

This paper examines the impact of Medicaid expansion on uninsured rates across states, using a difference-in-differences framework. I find significant reductions in uninsurance, particularly among low-income adults, with suggestive evidence of improved access to care.
